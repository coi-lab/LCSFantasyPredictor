🏆 LCS Fantasy Official Rules & System Guide

Everything you need to know about building rosters, scoring points, and week-to-week budget dynamics.

Creating Your Team

Sign up using your Riot ID, then name your team, pick a logo, and select a tricode. Each manager starts with a budget of 100 Gold.

🗓️ Roster Locks: Starting January 13th, 3PM PT, use your budget to build your roster. Rosters lock strictly before the start of the first game of each week.

Points are earned from your players' real-world performance in official LCS matches over the weekend. Results are calculated the day after the last weekend game.

Eligibility & Regions

Available in all regions except Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Crimea, Sudan, Venezuela, Syria, Greece, Italy, China, Quebec, Taiwan, Philippines, Hong Kong, Macau, Vietnam, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Myanmar, or where prohibited by law. Requires valid Riot Account and Summoner Name for rewards.

How Budget Updates From Week to Week

Your team budget directly expands or contracts based on the performance and market price changes of the players you selected!

⚡ Key Rule Mechanism: Direct Capital Appreciation Transfer
When a player on your roster plays exceptionally well over the weekend, their market cost increases. Your team budget for the following week increases by the exact amount that player's price went up!
1

Starting 100 Gold Budget

Every manager starts Week 1 with 100 Gold to assign players to their 5 player positions + 1 coach slot.

2

Player Price Recalculation

Each week, player market values adjust up or down based on their actual weekend fantasy performance vs. price benchmarks.

3

Direct Budget Growth

If Player A's price rises by +3.5 Gold, your total team budget for next week increases by +3.5 Gold (e.g. 100g → 103.5g)!

4

Roster Unlock & Upgrades

When rosters unlock at the start of the week, spend your newly expanded budget to purchase higher-tier superstars!

📊 Concrete Budget Growth Example

In Week 1, you draft a sleeper Mid laner for 14.0 Gold. Over the weekend, they pop off with a Penta Kill and 2 Wins. Their market price jumps from 14.0 Gold to 17.5 Gold (+3.5 Gold Gain).
For Week 2, your overall team purchasing budget automatically increases from 100.0 Gold to 103.5 Gold! Picking breakout players early rewards you with a larger budget to build super-teams in later weeks.

Scoring Mechanics

Each player's points are averaged across their games played on a weekend to account for variable series lengths (Bo1, Bo3, Bo5). Your final weekly score is the aggregate of your players' weekly averages.

Basic Events

  • Kills+1.5 pts
  • Assists+1.0 pt
  • Deaths-1.0 pt
  • Creep Score (CS)+0.01 / CS
  • First Blood+1.0 pt

Performance Bonuses

  • Kill Participation ≥ 70%+2.0 pts
  • Triple Kill+2.0 pts
  • Quadra Kill+3.0 pts
  • Penta Kill+5.0 pts
  • 10+ Kills+3.0 pts
  • Damage Share ≥ 30%+3.0 pts
  • Victory+1.0 pt
  • Stomping Victory (10k+ gold or <27m)+2.0 pts
  • Perfect Score (0 deaths & KDA ≥5)+3.0 pts
  • Gold Advantage @ 14:00+1 pt / 1000g

Stolen Objective Bonuses NEW IN SPLIT 3

Applied to the pro player who steals the objective:

🐉 Stolen Dragon+2 pts
👾 Stolen Rift Herald+2 pts
👑 Stolen Baron+4 pts
🔥 Stolen Elder Dragon+4 pts

Role-Specific Scoring

TOP LANE

  • Solo Kill: +1.0 pt
  • Damage Share ≥ 25%: +2.0 pts
  • Tank Bonus (≥25% team damage taken): +2.0 pts

JUNGLE

  • Team Gets 4+ Dragons: +1.5 pts
  • Baron Secured: +2.0 pts / Baron
  • Kill Participation ≥ 75%: +2.0 pts

MID LANE

  • Damage Share ≥ 30%: +3.0 pts
  • CS/Min ≥ 10 @ 15 min: +1.5 pts

BOT LANE (ADC)

  • CS/Min ≥ 10 @ 15 min: +1.5 pts
  • Damage/Min ≥ 1000: +1.0 pt

SUPPORT

  • 10+ Assists: +2.0 pts
  • Kill Participation ≥ 75%: +2.0 pts
  • First Dragon: +1.5 pts
  • Vision Score: Points equal to VSPM

Champion Prediction Multiplier

Correctly predicting a champion a player uses applies a multiplier for that game (highest non-cumulative multiplier applies based on prior split rounds):

  • Predicting champion not played in player's role:x1.7 Multiplier
  • Predicting champion not played before by that player:x1.5 Multiplier
  • Predicting champion already played by that player:x1.3 Multiplier

Variety Buff NEW IN SPLIT 3

Rosters with players from more teams earn a global percentage point bonus:

6 Teams: +25%
5 Teams: +20%
4 Teams: +15%
3 Teams: +10%
2 Teams: +5%
1 Team: No bonus

Coaches NEW IN SPLIT 3

In 2026, assign a Head Coach to your roster! The coach's score is calculated as the average fantasy score of that coach's LCS team roster.

Leaderboards & Competition

Your team automatically competes in the official LCS League and your chosen Favorite Team League upon registration. You can also join or create unlimited custom private leagues for friends and streamers using a single shared weekly roster.
