# Macro Strategy, Lane Priority, & Objective Win Conditions

## Overview

Pro teams draft champion compositions to match their intended early-game vs late-game macro win condition.

---

## 1. Early Lane Priority & Objective Stacking Strategy

* **Objective Focus**: Stacking early Void Grubs, Herald, and Dragons on cooldown ($< 15$ mins).
* **Draft Pattern**:
  - Teams select 2–3 early-push, winning-lane matchups (e.g. Varus + Karma bot, Jayce mid).
  - Guarantees early river and jungle priority for the jungler to secure objectives uncontested.

---

## 2. Late-Game Scaling & Teamfight Strategy

* **Objective Focus**: Conceding early dragons to stall for 25+ minute Baron and Elder teamfights.
* **Draft Pattern**:
  - Teams pick weakside solo lanes (e.g. K'Sante top, Smolder/Azir mid) and scaling hypercarries.
  - Prioritizes frontline tanks, disengage, and late-game damage scaling over early lane push.

---

## 3. Lane Priority Feature Matrix

* **Lane Push Rate at 15 mins**: Champion average minion push and tower damage before 15 mins.
* **Matchup Gold/CS Diff at 15 mins**: Head-to-head lane differential.
* **Team First-Dragon & First-Grub Share**: Historical rate at which a team secures first objectives.
