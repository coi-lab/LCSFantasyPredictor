# Draft Phase Order & Strategic Intent Theory

## Overview

Pro League of Legends drafts follow a 2-Phase structure (Picks 1–3 + Bans 1–3, followed by Bans 4–5 + Picks 4–5). The draft order dictates a team's strategic choices at each step.

---

## 1. Phase 1 (Bans 1–3, Picks 1–3): Power Pick Securing & Anchor Locking

* **First Pick (Blue 1) Priority**:
  - Blue 1 is reserved for **S-Tier Power Champions** on the patch (e.g., S-tier Orianna, Rumble, Kalista) or flex picks that can go into 2+ roles.
  - High win-rate, high-presence champions that force the opponent to react.
* **Anchor Pair Securing**:
  - Picks 2–3 (Red 1-2 & Blue 2-3) are used to lock in **Piece #1 or Piece #2 of core Anchor Pairs** (e.g., Bot Duo Lucian + Nami, Mid/Jungle Vi + Ahri).

---

## 2. Phase 2 (Bans 4–5): Target-Banning Star Players & Role Denial

* **Role Denial Bans**:
  - If a team's star player (e.g., Bin in Top lane or Chovy in Mid) has NOT picked in Phase 1, the opponent uses Bans 4–5 to **target-ban their comfort champion pool** before Phase 2 picks.
  - If Team A locks Top in Phase 1 while Team B hasn't, Team A bans out Team B's best remaining Top laners in Phase 2.

---

## 3. Phase 2 (Picks 4–5): Counter Picks & Matchup Optimization

* **Red 5 (Last Pick) Counter Pick**:
  - Red 5 (the final pick in the draft) is the ultimate **Counter Pick Slot**.
  - Teams save Red 5 for a hard lane counter (e.g., Top lane Renekton into K'Sante, Mid lane Sylas into Sejuani/Malphite) to guarantee a winning lane matchup.
* **Matchup Optimization**:
  - Picks 4–5 round out team composition needs (e.g., adding a frontline tank if the team drafted 3 squishy carries in Phase 1).
